Our Story

Built on Trust. Refined by Experience.

Since 1987, APEX Coal & Energy Resources has been at the heart of India's industrial energy supply chain. What started as a regional coal distribution firm in Mumbai has grown into a national powerhouse, serving over 500 companies across steel, cement, power generation, and manufacturing sectors.

We don't just supply coal — we provide a full spectrum of energy materials, from high-grade anthracite to sustainable biomass alternatives. Our commitment to consistent quality, on-time delivery, and deep market expertise has made us the preferred partner for India's most demanding industries.
